Common TV Issues We Fix

With 35 years of experience, we've seen and fixed every TV problem imaginable

TV Screen is Blank

No display but power light is on - Backlight or T-Con board issue

TV Not Switching On

No response when pressing power button - Power supply issue

Auto Switch Off/On

TV turns off or on automatically - Overheating or motherboard issue

HDMI Port Issues

No signal from connected devices - Port damage or board issue

Horizontal/Vertical Lines

Lines appearing on screen - Panel or ribbon cable issue

Squashed Images

Display distortion or aspect ratio issues - Settings or board problem
